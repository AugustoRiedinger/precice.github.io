Welcome to preCICE

The Coupling Library for Partitioned Multi-Physics Simulations.

Get started   Download v2.1.1   Star on Github  

preCICE is an open-source coupling library for partitioned multi-physics simulations, including, but not restricted to fluid-structure interaction and conjugate heat transfer simulations.

Partitioned means that preCICE couples existing programs/solvers capable of simulating a subpart of the complete physics involved in a simulation. This allows for the high flexibility that is needed to keep a decent time-to-solution for complex multi-physics scenarios.

The software offers convenient methods for transient equation coupling, communication, and data mapping.

Academic at Heart, 100% Free Software

preCICE is developed at the Chair of Scientific Computing at the Technical University of Munich and at the Institute for Parallel and Distributed Systems at the University of Stuttgart.

You can cite the preCICE library using the following paper. Please also consider citing the adapters you use. You can find the respective references in the documentation of our adapters and in our literature guide.

Tried and Tested by a Global Community

Through the community-driven development approach preCICE has become the leading coupling library for multi-physics simulations both in research and practice. preCICE is in active use on all continents apart from Antarctica. The wide variety of application fields include ranges from fluid simulation to austronautics, automotive manufacturing, aerodynamics, wind energy, biomechanics, biomimetics and many more.
